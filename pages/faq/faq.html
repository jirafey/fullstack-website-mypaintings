FAQ

▶ What is myp@intings?
Myp@intings is an online platform that connects artists with hotels and businesses to showcase and sell original artwork. It supports both custom orders and curated collections.
▶ How do hotels benefit?
Hotels can enhance their interior design with unique, rotating artwork while supporting independent artists. It also adds a personalized guest experience.
▶ Are there any fees?
There are no upfront fees to join the platform. A small commission is taken only when an artwork is sold.
▶ How can I sell my artwork on the platform?
Create an account, upload your artworks with descriptions and prices, and wait for buyers to place orders. The platform handles payment and shipping support.
▶ Can I order a custom artwork?
Yes, you can request a custom piece by contacting the artist through their profile. Simply describe your vision and agree on details before ordering.
▶ How is the artwork shipped?
Artworks are securely packaged and shipped via trusted courier services with tracking provided. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary by location.
▶ How can I contact customer support?
You can reach customer support through the contact form on our website or by emailing mpcontact@mypaintings.com or using form on our Contact page. We respond within 24–48 hours.
▶ How does the platform work?
Artists upload their work, buyers browse and purchase, and the platform manages transactions and logistics. It’s a seamless process for both creators and customers.
▶ What features are available on the website?
Features include artist profiles, search filters, direct messaging, order tracking, and custom request tools. The site is optimized for both desktop and mobile use.
